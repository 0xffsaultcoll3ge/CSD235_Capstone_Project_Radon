About Project Radon

Project Radon is designed to help you place safer and more secure NHL sports bets. The idea emerged from the need to replace emotional betting strategies with data-driven predictions, leveraging artificial intelligence to achieve mathematical precision. Initially made as a passion project, the concept evolved into a project for sale in 2025. Project Radon has your back to help leverage sports betting choices.
